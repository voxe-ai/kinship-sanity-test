Outdoor Venue

The Yard

The Yard is Kinship Landing’s outdoor event space in downtown Colorado Springs, designed for gatherings that feel casual, connected, and unforced. It’s where cocktail hours turn into conversations, welcome parties stretch into the evening, and teams unwind after a full day of meetings or retreats.

Perfect for wedding receptions, rehearsal dinners, cocktail hours, networking events, and group celebrations, The Yard pairs naturally with The Greenhaus for an indoor–outdoor experience that moves with your people. Fire pits, flexible seating, and space to mingle make it feel more like a great backyard than a traditional venue.

Step outside for fresh air and mountain light, then settle back in with a drink in hand and nowhere else to be. With downtown Colorado Springs all around and Pikes Peak just beyond, The Yard is an easy place to gather, celebrate, and stay awhile.
