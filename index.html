Kinship is Your Guide

Named Best Hotel in Colorado Springs by Springs Magazine’s People’s Choice Awards and recognized as a Michelin Selected Hotel for 2025, Kinship Landing is a boutique basecamp for exploring the Front Range in Colorado Springs.

Landing in a new place feels different when it doesn’t feel new at all.
At Kinship, you’re not just checking in, you’re being welcomed. We’re your friendly local boutique hotel basecamp in downtown Colorado Springs, the kind of place where recommendations come with real opinions, favorite coffee orders, and a “tell them we sent you.” Think of us as your insider guide to the places, people, and moments that make Colorado Springs feel alive.

Whether you’re here to eat and drink your way through the neighborhood, explore local art and culture just outside our doors, or head out early for hiking, biking, and wide-open views on Pikes Peak, we’ll help you experience Colorado Springs in a way that feels authentic, easy, and entirely your own.

One of our favorite things? Helping you have an amazing time.
We’re always happy to point you in the right direction, and every now and then, we might even join you along the way. Around here, hospitality is personal, and you’re already part of our circle.

Gather Together

Kinship Landing offers unique spaces to gather, celebrate, and meet in the heart of downtown Colorado Springs. From company retreats and board meetings to weddings, reunions, and celebrations, our event spaces are designed to bring people together in a relaxed, welcoming setting. With boutique hotel rooms, flexible meeting spaces, and walkable access to downtown restaurants, nightlife, and attractions, Kinship makes it easy to turn a meeting or event into a full Colorado Springs experience.

Anchoring the public first floor of Kinship Landing is Homa, our craft café and bar. We took fresh, locally sourced ingredients and combined them with our favorite globally inspired dishes to offer nutrient dense and delightfully delicious food and drinks to fuel your adventures from sunrise to late night.

Best of the Springs - Silver Winner!

Homa Café + Bar is proud to be voted the Best of the Springs Silver Winner for Best Hotel Restaurant by the Colorado Springs community.

Designed for the hungry and the healthy alike, locals, hotel guests, and old friends gather and enjoy hearty sandwiches, flavorful whole grain or salad bowls, small bites like our signature hand pies, fresh soups, and entrees or brunch and breakfast packed full of yummy goodness. Share it all with a pint of local craft beer, freshly roasted coffee, in house custom cocktails, or the perfect glass of wine, kombucha, or an ice-oat milk latte.
